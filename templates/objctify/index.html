Objctify Me

Use Deep Learning to rate and improve* your selfies

About:

Objctify Me is a fun side project, that uses Deep Learning to analyse your facial structure, how it differs from others, and then changes your facial structure slightly to create a new and improved image. The changes the AI algorithm makes are typically subtle, so that you can put your best face forward while still being recognisable, handy for slight changes to your online dating or social media profiles.
